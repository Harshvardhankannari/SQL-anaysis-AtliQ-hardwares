# 🚀 Sales Analytics Project | SQL

## 📌 Project Overview

This project demonstrates end-to-end data analysis using SQL to solve real-world business problems in sales and customer analytics. It focuses on extracting actionable insights from structured datasets to support decision-making in pricing, customer strategy, and product growth.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze **product growth trends** across fiscal years  
- Identify **high-performing segments and divisions**  
- Evaluate **customer discount behavior** and its impact  
- Track **monthly and quarterly sales performance**  
- Measure **channel-wise revenue contribution**  
- Detect **cost extremes** in manufacturing  

---

## 💡 Key Insights Delivered

- 📈 **Product Expansion**: Observed a significant increase in unique products from 2020 to 2021, indicating business scaling  
- 🏆 **Top Segments**: Certain segments contributed disproportionately to product growth  
- 💰 **Revenue Drivers**: Identified the most impactful sales channels contributing to overall revenue  
- 🎯 **Customer Strategy**: Highlighted top customers receiving high discounts, useful for margin optimization  
- 📊 **Sales Trends**: Detected seasonal patterns in monthly and quarterly sales  
- ⚖️ **Cost Optimization**: Found products with extreme manufacturing costs for potential cost control
